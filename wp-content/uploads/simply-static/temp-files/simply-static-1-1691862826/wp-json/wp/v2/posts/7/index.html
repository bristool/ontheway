A security alert Saturday prompted the evacuation of three floors of the Eiffel Tower in central Paris, France’s most emblematic symbol which drew 6.2 million visitors last year.<\/p>\n

SETE, the body which runs the site, said bomb disposal experts as well as police were scouring the area, including a restaurant located on one of the floors.<\/p>\n

“It’s a usual procedure in this kind of situation which however is rare,” a spokeswoman said.<\/p>\n

Visitors were evacuated from both the three floors and the square under the monument shortly after 1:30 pm (1130 GMT).<\/p>\n

Construction work on the tower began in January 1887 and was finished on March 31, 1889. It received two million visitors during the World’s Fair of 1889.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
